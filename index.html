OUR AIM


Food is a common necessaity for humans to survive on this planet.Even though
there are lots of food produced we waste a major percent of it ,thus our goal
is to reduce the wastage and help the economically weaker .Making assure that
no one goes hungry is what we as a society believe in collectively. Acts such as
these will eliminate the continuous cycle of starvation, undernourishment, and
suffering, enabling the poor to live a better life.As it is said in the sacred books
"अन्नदाता सुखी भव".It is a prayer that the person who feeds you or anyone for that
matter,is always happy and contend.So we as a Charity call for the citizens of this
nation to join our hands to eradicate hunger from our country

REDUCE THE FOOD WASTAGE

A Lot of food is being wasted daily in many occassion such as marraiges and other functions.The left overs are either dumped in the sewages or disposed in the garbages. Instead of preparing the exccessive food ,it must be prepared on the basis of requirement. And in case if there are any left overs do let us know so that a kind guesture from your side helps us to feed a empty stomach and bring a smile on a poor face
